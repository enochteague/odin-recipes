Savory Garlic Marinated Steaks

If you're anything like me, you love a good steak. If you got a grill, make some of this. This recipe will have you craving more!

Ingredients

  • 2 half pound ribe-eye steaks
  • 1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon liquid smoke flavoring
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper

Directions!

  1. Place steaks in a shallow glass dish and set aside
  2. Mix together vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, honey, olive oil, black pepper, Worcestershire sauce, onion powder, salt, liquid smoke, and cayenne pepper in a medium bowl until well combined. Pour over steaks and rub into a coat. Refrigerate for 1-2 days.
  3. Preheat grill to medium high to high, lightly oil the grate.
  4. Cook steaks about 7 minutes per side or until the meat is 140 degrees.
