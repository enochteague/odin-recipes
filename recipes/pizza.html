Pizza From Scratch!

Have you ever been to your favorite italian restaurant and wished you could just take the delicious pizza home? Well, follow these directions and you'll have a delicious pizza made fresh from scratch!

Ingredients

Sauce

  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 can (12 Oz) tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil, crushed
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Crust

  • 3 1/4 cups all-purpose flour, or more as needed
  • 1/4 oz pizza crust yeast
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 1/3 cups very warm water
  • 1/3 cup oil

Toppings

  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella, or to taste
  • 6 oz pepperoni

Directions!

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F or 200 degrees C. Grease two 12 inch pizza pans.
  2. Make sauce by mixing water, tomato paste, oregano, basil, garlic powder, onion powder, sugar, salt, and pepper into a medium bowl until smooth. Set aside.
  3. Make crust by combining 2 cups flour, yeast, sugar, and salt into a large bowl. Add oil and water, mix until well blended. Gradually add flour until a soft, sticky dough forms.
  4. Transfer dough to a floured surface, knead until smooth and elastic, add flour as needed.
  5. Divide dough in half. Lightly flour hands, then pat each piece onto prepared pans.
  6. Top dough with sauce, cheese, and pepperoni
  7. Bake 18-20 minutes or until crust is brown and cheese is bubbly. Rotate the pans from top to bottom rack halfway through.
